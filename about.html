About Us

A small patriotic dream and unbounded love for our City of Joy – Kolkata helped create the concept of Kollolini Kolkata - not a Hotel but a cosy budget friendly home away from home which suits the needs of every traveller. Our ideology is “ ATITHI DEVO BHAVO” – Guest is God. So we believe in treating them with the best of what we have in terms of hospitality, amenities, standard and quality. We house 7 spacious rooms which includes double bedded super deluxe rooms, super deluxe single rooms, economy deluxe twin bedded rooms as well as economy deluxe double rooms. Rooms are beautifully furnished with wardrobes, sofas, tables, TV, AC, tea maker, bed side table, dressing unit and each room has a touch of love, affection and care. Amidst the hustle bustle of the city life Kollolini Kolkata proudly boasts about its surrounding peace and serenity in spite of its close proximity to the Passport Office, Acropolis Mall, Kasba Industrial Estate, Science City, Hospitals ( Ruby, Desun, Fortis, Medica, RTIICS- Devi Shetty, Peerless, AMRI, etc). Other than being surrounded by famous regular restaurant chains, Kollolini Kolkata tingles the tummy of her guests with the best of home cooked delicious authentic Bengali Cuisine which if not tried would mean missing the most heavenly experience.
