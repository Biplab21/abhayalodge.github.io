About Us

Established in 2017, Lodge Avoya is located centrally on Bagmundi road, where from you can get a serenic view of the Ayodhya hills sitting by your hotel room balcony while taking a sip of your morning tea. We also provide personalised packages and necessary arrangements for guests who want to cook their own food, also for corporate outings. We have parking lot for tourist vehicles too.
